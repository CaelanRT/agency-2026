# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

**Procurement Risk Radar (Alberta MVP)**

---

## 1. Overview

Procurement Risk Radar is a lightweight analytics and AI-assisted dashboard that helps users explore Alberta government spending and quickly identify potential procurement risks such as vendor concentration and reliance on sole-source contracts.

The MVP focuses on **one core workflow**:

> “For a selected ministry, who receives the most funding, and are there any risk signals?”
> 

---

## 2. Goals

### Primary Goal

Enable users to:

- Select a ministry
- View top funding recipients
- Identify concentration and sole-source risk
- Receive a plain-English AI-generated summary

### Success Criteria (Hackathon)

- Working UI with ministry filter
- Backend queries BigQuery successfully
- Displays top recipients (contracts)
- Displays sole-source amounts
- Generates AI summary
- Demoable end-to-end in under 60 seconds

---

## 3. Scope

### In Scope (MVP)

- Alberta **contracts**
- Alberta **sole source contracts**
- Ministry-level filtering
- Top recipients by spend
- Basic risk indicators
- AI-generated summary

### Out of Scope

- Complex entity resolution
- Non-profit classification
- Grants integration (optional stretch)
- Multi-agent orchestration
- Fraud detection claims

---

## 4. Data Sources

### Primary Tables

- `ab_contracts`
    - ministry
    - recipient
    - amount
    - display_fiscal_year
- `ab_sole_source`
    - recipient
    - amount
    - ministry (if available)

### Optional (Stretch)

- `ab_grants`

---

## 5. Core User Flow

1. User opens app
2. User selects a **ministry** from dropdown
3. System fetches:
    - Top recipients (contracts)
    - Their total spend
    - Their sole-source totals
4. UI displays:
    - Table of top recipients
    - Basic metrics
5. AI generates:
    - Risk summary for selected ministry

---

## 6. Features

### 6.1 Ministry Selector

- Dropdown populated from distinct ministries in `ab_contracts`

---

### 6.2 Top Recipients Table

Columns:

- Recipient
- Total Contract Spend
- Sole Source Spend
- % of Total Spend (optional but recommended)

Limit:

- Top 10 recipients by total spend

---

### 6.3 Risk Indicators (Simple)

Computed values:

- Top vendor share (% of total ministry spend)
- Top 3 vendors share (optional)
- Total sole-source proportion

---

### 6.4 AI Risk Summary

### Input to AI:

- Top vendor
- Total spend
- Vendor share %
- Sole source totals

### Output:

Short paragraph:

Example:

> “Spending in this ministry is concentrated among a small number of vendors. The top vendor accounts for 62% of total contract value, with significant reliance on sole-source contracts, which may indicate reduced competition.”
>

## 9. Key SQL Queries

### Top Recipients

```sql
SELECT
  recipient,
  SUM(amount) AS total_spend
FROM `PROJECT.DATASET.ab_contracts`
WHERE ministry = @ministry
GROUP BY recipient
ORDER BY total_spend DESC
LIMIT 10;
```

---

### Sole Source Totals

```sql
SELECT
  recipient,
  SUM(amount) AS sole_source_total
FROM `PROJECT.DATASET.ab_sole_source`
GROUP BY recipient;
```

---

### Join (Backend merge)

- Join on `recipient`
- Compute share = recipient_total / ministry_total

## 11. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
| --- | --- |
| Dirty or inconsistent recipient names | Accept as-is for MVP |
| Missing joins between datasets | Show partial data |
| Time constraints | Focus on contracts only |
| AI hallucination | Only summarize provided data |

## 14. Key Insight

This product does not attempt to detect fraud.

It surfaces patterns that:

- reduce competition
- increase dependency
- may warrant further investigation
